# 30-60-90 Day Training & Adoption Plan

**Agent:** @Change-Management-Coach (Phase 2 Lead)
**Program Phase:** Primary focus Days 31-60, continues through Day 90
**Goal:** Ensure developers and testers can safely and effectively use AI tools throughout program execution.

## Program-Aligned Training Strategy

### Phase 1: Planning & Preparation (Days 1-30)
**Focus:** Foundation and readiness assessment
- [ ] Identify training needs and audience segments
- [ ] Develop training materials and curricula
- [ ] Select pilot team participants
- [ ] Conduct change readiness assessment
- [ ] Create communication plan
- **Deliverable:** Training plan approved, materials ready

### Phase 2: Pilot & Validation (Days 31-60)
**Focus:** Intensive pilot team training and adoption tracking
- [ ] Week 1: AI basics, data privacy, tool overview
- [ ] Week 2-3: Role-based training (dev workflows, QA workflows)
- [ ] Week 4-6: Applied practice with sandbox projects
- [ ] Daily: Office hours and coaching
- [ ] Weekly: Adoption metrics tracking and reporting
- **Deliverable:** Pilot team fully trained, adoption validated

### Phase 3: Transition & Scale (Days 61-90)
**Focus:** Team-wide rollout and reinforcement
- [ ] Team-wide training sessions (by role)
- [ ] Champions program launch (2-3 per team)
- [ ] Peer learning and knowledge sharing
- [ ] Continuous improvement based on feedback
- [ ] Success celebration and reinforcement
- **Deliverable:** Full team operational, metrics meeting targets

## Target Audiences

- **Developers:** Code generation, review, refactoring
- **QA/Testers:** Test generation, triage, automation
- **Managers:** Metrics, governance, team coaching
- **Security/Compliance:** Policy enforcement, monitoring

## Adoption Metrics (Tracked by @Performance-Optimization-Agent)

**Phase 2 Targets (Days 31-60):**
- Active users: 80%+ of pilot team
- Usage frequency: 4+ days/week
- Time saved per task: 20%+ improvement
- Quality scores: Maintain or improve baseline
- Satisfaction survey: 7/10 average

**Phase 3 Targets (Days 61-90):**
- Active users: 90%+ of full team
- Usage frequency: Daily for core workflows
- Time saved per task: 30%+ improvement
- Quality scores: 10%+ improvement over baseline
- Satisfaction survey: 8/10 average

## Champions Program

- Identify 2-3 champions per team (Day 30)
- Weekly knowledge sharing sessions (Phase 2-3)
- Feedback loop to @Program-Manager for improvements
- Recognition and celebration of successes

## Program Deliverables

**Phase 1 (Days 1-30):**
- [ ] Training materials and recordings
- [ ] Role-based job aids and quick reference
- [ ] Communication plan and schedule
- [ ] Change readiness assessment report

**Phase 2 (Days 31-60):**
- [ ] Pilot training completion certificates
- [ ] Weekly adoption metrics dashboards
- [ ] FAQ and troubleshooting guide (living document)
- [ ] Mid-program feedback and adjustments

**Phase 3 (Days 61-90):**
- [ ] Full team training completion
- [ ] Final adoption metrics and ROI validation
- [ ] Lessons learned and best practices (@Case-Study-Documenter)
- [ ] Continuous improvement roadmap
