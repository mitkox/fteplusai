Program Phases

90-Day Execution Roadmap

A structured approach to vendor replacement with clear milestones and decision points.

01
Days 1-30

Planning & Preparation

Build the business case, select tools, and prepare your team for the transition.

  • Executive alignment and budget approval
  • Tool evaluation and selection
  • Pilot team identification
  • Risk assessment and mitigation planning
  • Initial training and setup
02
Days 31-60

Pilot & Validation

Prove the AI approach works with real projects in a controlled environment.

  • Pilot team execution with AI tools
  • Quality and productivity measurement
  • Cost validation against benchmarks
  • Parallel run with vendor
  • Go/No-Go decision for phase 3
03
Days 61-90

Transition & Scale

Full team rollout, vendor cutover, and post-transition optimization.

  • Team-wide training and deployment
  • Vendor contract wind-down
  • Knowledge transfer completion
  • Full production cutover
  • Post-transition optimization

Creating a 30-60-90 Program Plan 
# Invoke the Program Manager agent to create your comprehensive plan
@Program-Manager Create a 30-60-90 day vendor replacement program plan

Context: We currently use [vendor] for [scope]
Constraints: [data policy], [timeline], [no downtime]
Output: Phase 1/2/3 plan + milestones + phase gates + risks + stakeholder map
Financial Analysis Example 
# Calculate ROI and TCO for vendor replacement
@ROI-Calculator Create a cost comparison between our offshore vendor and AI-augmented FTEs

Current vendor: 3 developers at $50/hour
Team size: 5 FTEs
Calculate: 3-year ROI, payback period, and TCO
